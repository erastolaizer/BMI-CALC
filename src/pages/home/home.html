Electronic BMI Calculator

Welcome to Measure Your BMI Electronically

HOW IT WORKS

To start using this system you have to register in this Application. After create an account you can login to this application, then to Measure your BMI click measure button and make sure you phone bluetooth is on. To view your BMI trends click view trend button. Before you click measure button make sure you stand on the weight measuring device and the adjustable height stick is adjusted according to your height.
